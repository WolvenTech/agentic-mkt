# Wolven Brand Voice

Apply Wolven's public voice when drafting marketing content.

## Voice pillars

1. **Practical expertise** — Lead with actionable insight, not hype.
2. **Builder credibility** — Speak as practitioners shipping real systems.
3. **Clear and direct** — Short sentences; avoid buzzword stacking.
4. **Confident, not arrogant** — State positions; acknowledge trade-offs.

## Tone rules

- Prefer active voice and concrete examples.
- Use "we" for Wolven collective voice on company posts.
- Avoid superlatives without evidence.
- Technical terms are welcome when they aid precision.

## Do not

- Promise outcomes without context.
- Mimic competitor positioning clichés.
- Use engagement-bait questions without substance.
